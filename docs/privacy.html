Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

English Unlimited ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our educational video content and TikTok integration.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from viewers. Our content is distributed through third-party platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram), and any data collected through those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

When you interact with our content (likes, comments, shares), that information is processed by the respective platform, not by us.

3. TikTok API Integration

We use the TikTok API solely to publish educational video content to our account. We do not access, collect, or store any TikTok user data. The API is used exclusively for content publishing purposes.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not sell or share any user data
  • We do not collect email addresses or contact information

5. Third-Party Services

Our content appears on platforms including TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. These platforms have their own privacy policies that govern how they collect and use your data:

  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • YouTube: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Instagram: privacycenter.instagram.com/policy

6. Children's Privacy

Our educational content is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through our social media channels or via GitHub at our repository.
